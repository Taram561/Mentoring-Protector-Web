The First Line of Defense

Threats often come through the browser. Our extension acts as an invisible shield, analyzing websites before they can load and cause harm.

Phishing Blocking

Scammers create copies of bank and social media websites to steal your passwords. The extension checks every domain against our constantly updated database and analyzes the site's age. If "vk.com-login.ru" was registered yesterday, it will be immediately blocked.

Malicious Script Analysis

Some websites quietly use your CPU resources for cryptocurrency mining or try to intercept credit card data. We analyze the page code in real time and stop the execution of suspicious scripts.

In-Browser Education

Like the main client, the extension doesn't just silently block the site. It shows you a page with a detailed explanation of what exactly gave the scammers away, helping you develop digital hygiene.
